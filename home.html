Welcome to COMP-10001 PASS Sessions

My name is Alwin Lee, and I will be your Peer Assisted Study Session (PASS) Leader this semester. These sessions are designed to boost your success in COMP 10001 through structured group learning.

Why You Should Attend PASS

Improve Academic Performance

Students who attend PASS statistically achieve higher final grades compared to those who do not attend.

Increase
Confidence

Sessions are designed to boost your confidence in the course material through engaging group activities and dynamic discussions.

Encouraging Environment

Sessions are inclusive, encouraging students to participate and ask questions in a judgment-free environment to enhance their learning experience.
